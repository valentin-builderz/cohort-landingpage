The Shift

Every skill that fits in a process doc is getting commoditized. What's left is what AI cannot describe.

That is not a crisis for everyone. For the people who know where their genius lives, it is the best possible market condition.

Most people will try to outrun this with more output.

More tools. More content. More efficiency. But if AI is compressing the value of generic competence, working faster is not enough.

The people who come out ahead will not be the best prompt engineers. They will be the ones who know what is actually theirs, then build something the market can feel before the conversation even starts.

FAQ

No. AI is one layer, not the whole thing. You cannot use AI as a multiplier if you don't know what you are multiplying. The Zone of Genius work comes first.

Do I need a big audience for this to make sense?

No. This is not about follower counts. It is about becoming the obvious choice for the right people. You need to be known to the few who matter, not the many who won't buy anyway.

Who is this best for?

Consultants, advisors, agency owners, operators, and founder-led builders who already have real ability and can feel generic positioning getting more dangerous.

Why an application instead of open checkout?

Because the room matters. A cohort is only as good as the people in it. Applications keep the work specific and the conversations worth having.

What do I leave with after 8 weeks?

A clear Zone of Genius. A positioning and messaging system built around it. AI workflows that amplify it. And a higher standard for what you are building toward.
